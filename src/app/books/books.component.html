NAAM KI MAHIMA - GLORIES OF THE HOLY NAME

"HARE KRISHNA HARE KRISHNA KRISHNA KRISHNA HARE HARE

HARE HARE RAMA HARE RAMA RAMA RAMA HARE HARE"

In every era, there are different ways of realising God which are specific to that time period. However, in the present age of Kaliyug, it has become very easy to realise God by simply chanting the Holy names of Lord and participating in Hari Naam Sankirtan (The congregational chanting). There is no hard and fast rule to chant the Holy names of Krishna. The process of chanting is beyond the boundaries of country, time, circumstances, rituals and age. There is no difference between The Holy names of God and God Himself. Therefore, in this dark age of Kaliyug, the glories and the benefits of the Holy names are limitless. This booklet “The Glories of The Holy Name” is an effort to arise the Love of God, faith and trust within the core of your heart so that you surrender yourself to the transcendental sound of the Holy name and embark your journey back to home, back to Godhead.

— Shri Madhusudan Bapuji
